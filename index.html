    Our UX Principles

    As UX designers at PLOS, this is what we value:

    Understanding

    Science doesn’t have a goal; scientists do. We design for people who have feelings, needs, and goals.

    Consistency

    Consistency = trust. We are consistent with our designs, patterns, interactions and the language we use. 

    Data

    We believe that using data to inform our design decisions will help us get to the right solution faster. We use data to validate ideas, scrutinize assumptions, identify opportunities, and measure impact.
